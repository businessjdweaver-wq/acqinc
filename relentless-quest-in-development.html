👋 Welcome to Your Franchise

Start by creating or activating contracts on the Contracts screen. Tap + Add to make a new task, then use the gold button on a task whenever you complete progress on it.

Visit the Recruitment Office from the Party screen to hire full adventurers or budget hirelings. Your Founder begins the company, but extra hands help you tackle larger jobs.

On the Quest screen, choose a quest, assign a valid party, and begin the adventure. Regular quests require at least one Level 1+ full adventurer. When they return, use franchise gold or each hero's purse to buy gear from the Store.

Tip: archived contracts can stay in storage for occasional work, and favourites can still repopulate automatically at your reset time.
