# Year-1 Quest Reward Ramp Calendar (Visualization)

This planning map assumes:

- Starting gold on Day 1: **125 gp**
- Daily allocation: **400 gp/day**
- Balance target by end of Year 1:
  - 5 characters at level 20 (using recommended level-up pricing from prior analysis)
  - 3 high-level items per character (15 total)

## Target Cost Envelope

- Estimated total target spend: **251,250 gp**
  - Leveling (team): 71,250 gp
  - High-level items (15 @ 12,000): 180,000 gp
- Allocation-only yearly funds: **146,125 gp**
- Required quest-earned funds to hit target: **105,125 gp**

### Year Totals (3/day baseline)

- Total quests completed: **1,095**
- Total quest-earned GP: **185,685 gp**

At this baseline, the 251,250 gp target is reached around **October** (about **912 quests completed**).

## How Many Quests Must We Generate?

Use this as capacity planning for content generation:

- **Minimum completion target for the year (economic floor):** ~**620 quests**
  - Based on required quest GP (105,125) divided by ~170 gp average reward across the year.
- **Design target at 3/day cadence:** **1,095 quests/year**
- **Upper range at 6/day cadence:** **2,190 quests/year**

### Practical recommendation

- Ensure at least **1,100 quest opportunities/year** for a 3/day baseline.
- If you want players to operate in the 3–6/day band without repetition fatigue, plan for **~1,500–2,000 available quest opportunities** (or robust replayable templates).
